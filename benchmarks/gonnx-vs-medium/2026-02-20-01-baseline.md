## Analysis

Key observations:
- **0% overall win rate** across all 7 powers
- **8 draws** (11.4%) where the gonnx power survived to the year limit
- England held the most SCs on average (1.0 final), likely due to island isolation
- Turkey was the slowest to collapse (avg 2.5 SCs through 1906) but still 0 wins
- Russia collapsed fastest (0 SCs by 1906)
- Most games ended by 1915 with the gonnx power eliminated

This is expected: the policy network outputs a distribution over orders but picks greedily without any search or multi-step planning. The medium bot uses tactical evaluation with 1-3 ply lookahead and support injection, which significantly outperforms raw policy.
